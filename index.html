HERE ARE A FEW WAYS TO CONNECT

We strongly believe that following the steps Start. Connect. Serve are key in your spiritual growth. Walking through these steps is as simple as clicking a link for more information. We are happy to help you take the next step in your spiritual growth.

Join A Small Group

Our Digital Small Groups consist of 5 to 10 people that meet weekly to discuss scripture and share their lives together. Joining a Digital Small Group is the best way to connect with other people at TCC.

Weekly Devotional

Meet weekly on Sundays,While we are worshiping online many of our Life Group leaders are sending out encouraging devotional thoughts on social media and via email.

Serve Team

We strongly believe that members who serve are more connected to the TCC family. No matter what skills or interests you have, we have opportunities for you.
